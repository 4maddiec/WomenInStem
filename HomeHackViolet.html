Welcome to F*ck the Patriarchy!

This is our HackViolet project! Here we are trying to spread information on famous women in STEM and promote female empowerment. On this website we have provided information on famous historical figures in STEM. We have also provided quotes from real everyday women in STEM. We also hope to spread empowerment through women outside STEM categories to help promote female empowerment in all areas of society.
